# Estate map — stylized downtown skyscrapers

Distinctive non-Kenney towers that give the city centre its character. Sourced
from Poly Pizza, rendered via `MultiMatInstancedModel` (multi-material). Mixed
into `TOWERS`/`LANDMARK_TOWERS` in `src/pages/academy/cityModels.js`.
